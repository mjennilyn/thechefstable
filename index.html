The Chef's Table

Head Chef:
Neil Osama

Following the footsteps of his father Chef Neil Osama has fallen in love with the art of culinary at the age of five years old. Osama, worked under his father learning each technique and skill needed in a Master Chef. Osama, earned his degree in Hotel Management in 1992. To step away from his father's shadow he has established his own resturaunt The Chef's Table in 1998.

The Experience

We firmly believe that our kitchen is our home. Like any home each guest should be provided with nothing but the best hospitatlity and service. As our guest we expect you to feel at home in our kitchen. Our families goal is to assure you walk out of our kitchen in bliss and tranquility. Our Chef's will be preparing each dish before you allowing you to experience every attention to detail and design. Due to our unique style in dining, we are only able to cater up to 15 guest at each time. Please reserve 2-3 hours of your time to assure you are able to fulfill the full experience. Our family looks forward in dining with you.

Our Menu

Our Chef's believe each visit should be unique. Therefore, our menu changes on a daily basis. A five course meal will be served along with an optional wine pairing specifically selected by our partnered wine connoisseur to harmoniously match with each spice and flavor. Please inform our reservation host of any food allergies.
